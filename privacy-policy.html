Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Translate Screen Lite mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") developed by GF-Apps (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider").

The Application is provided as a Free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

1. No Personal Data Collection

The Application does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data or user content.

The Application does not require account registration and does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising identifiers.

2. Accessibility & Overlay Permissions

The Application requires the following permissions to function:

  • Accessibility Service Permission – used solely to detect and read on-screen text for translation purposes.
  • Display Over Other Apps (Overlay) Permission – used to display translated text visually over other applications.

The Accessibility Service is strictly used to analyze visible on-screen text in order to provide translation functionality.

No text, content, or personal data accessed through Accessibility is stored, recorded, transmitted, or shared.

Users may enable or disable these permissions at any time via device settings. If disabled, translation features will not function.

3. On-Device Processing (Google ML Kit)

The Application uses Google ML Kit for text recognition and translation.

All text recognition and translation processes occur locally on the user's device (offline).

The Application does not send recognized text or translated content to external servers.

An internet connection may be required only for downloading language models. Once downloaded, translation works offline.

4. Internet Usage

The Application may temporarily access the internet solely for downloading language models required for offline translation.

No user content is transmitted during translation.

5. Location Information

The Application does not collect precise or approximate location information.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The Application may rely on Google Play Services for essential app distribution and functionality. You may review Google's Privacy Policy here:

  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Since no personal data is collected, there is minimal risk to children's privacy.

8. Data Security

Because the Application does not collect or store personal data, there is no database of user information that could be accessed or breached.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
